Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2026

At Grow Baby Grow, we prioritize the privacy and security of your family's data. This policy outlines our commitment to your privacy.

1. Data Collection

Grow Baby Grow is designed as a **local-first** application. All developmental data, child profiles, and growth measurements are stored directly on your device using IndexedDB. We do not transmit this data to any external servers.

2. Use of Information

The information entered into the app is used solely for generating developmental reports, visualizing growth charts, and providing clinical alerts within the local environment of the app.

3. Local Storage

Since data is stored locally, deleting the app or clearing your browser cache (in PWA version) will permanently delete your data unless you have performed a manual backup/export.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party tracking or advertising services. We do not sell or share any information with third parties.

5. Consent

By using Grow Baby Grow, you consent to this local-only data storage model.
